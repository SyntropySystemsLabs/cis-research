Relationship to Broader Research Program

CIS functions as the foundational scientific layer underlying several related systems under development.

These include:

VerityCore
An epistemic operating system designed to:
• audit reasoning systems
• detect constraint violations
• support truth-seeking computational architectures.

CIS/IP
A proposed interoperability protocol enabling constraint-aware communication between reasoning systems across different AI models or institutional agents.

Institutional Constraint Modeling
Application of CIS principles to analyze how institutions behave as informational systems governed by:
• structural constraints
• incentive architectures
• informational flows.

Future Work
The CIS research program will continue to expand in several directions:
• formal mathematical models of constraint dynamics
• simulation environments for informational systems
• governance layers for AI reasoning systems
• interoperability frameworks across multiple models
